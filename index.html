doty·touch

Many aspiring Braille learners lack access to a Braille machine or stylus, making it difficult to practice and master the system. Traditional tools are expensive, rare, and impractical for everyday use, creating a barrier to Braille literacy.

This app solves that problem by simulating a real Braille machine, allowing users to type Braille using designated keys on their device. With real-time feedback and an intuitive design, it makes learning and practicing Braille accessible to anyone, anywhere—no special equipment needed.
